About Me

About Me

I joined the Computer Research Institute of Montreal in December 2019 following the completion of my masters in Electronic Business Technologies. I also hold a bachelor in Management Information Systems and Analytics. I have been involved in several projects, including emotion detection and the development of a content-based recommender system.

My main interests are related to data science and the applications of new algorithms in machine learning on time series and natural language processing tasks. Most recently, I have been concentrating on deep learning approaches and following the FastAI MOOC.
